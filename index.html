Splito – Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's exactly what we do (and don't do) with your data.

Last updated: May 2025

👋 Overview

Splito ("we", "us", or "our") is a personal finance app that helps you split bills and shared expenses with friends. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Splito mobile application ("App").

Splito does not require you to create an account, and all your expense data is stored locally on your device only. We do not have servers that store your personal information.

📷 Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device's camera solely to let you scan physical receipts. When you tap "Scan Receipt," the camera captures an image of the receipt so the App can read the items and amounts.

  • Camera images are processed on-device and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Receipt images are not stored permanently; they are used only during the active scanning session.
  • You can deny or revoke the camera permission at any time in your device's Settings without losing existing expense data.

📦 Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary for the App to function:

  • Expense data you enter (amounts, descriptions, participant names) – stored locally on your device using SQLite; never transmitted to us.
  • App preferences (theme setting, onboarding status) – stored locally using SharedPreferences.
  • Receipt images – used transiently during scanning; not persisted.
  • Files you choose to import (PDF receipts from your storage) – read locally only; not uploaded.

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.

📢 Advertising (Google AdMob)

Splito shows ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information automatically to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable, anonymous identifier)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Device type, OS version, and language
  • App interaction signals (e.g., ad clicks, impressions)

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can learn more and opt out of personalized ads at policies.google.com/technologies/ads .

On Android, you can reset or limit ad tracking in Settings → Google → Ads.

🔗 Third-Party Services

Splito integrates the following third-party SDKs:

  • Google AdMob – advertising. Privacy Policy
  • CunningDocumentScanner – on-device document scanning (no data leaves the device).

We do not integrate analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or any other tracking services.

🚫 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer any information about you to third parties. Your expense data never leaves your device.

The only third party that may receive data is Google AdMob, solely for the purpose of displaying ads, as described above.

👦 Children's Privacy

Splito is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

🗄️ Data Retention & Deletion

All expense data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all App data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing the App's data in your device's Settings. This permanently removes all stored information.

🔒 Security

Because your data is stored locally, it is protected by your device's own security mechanisms (screen lock, device encryption). We recommend keeping your device's OS updated and using a strong screen lock.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
